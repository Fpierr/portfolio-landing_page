About us

This Project was inspired bay Fanuel PIERRE, Software Engineering Student at HOLBERTON SCHOOL. This idea come from a significant probleme. Firstly by Brain Drain, due to globalization and migration when Millions of peoples haved moving for educational professional or personnal reasons, and the difficulties of pupils and students in their personnal or academic project and carrer guidance. Fanuel saw the necessity of having this skils exchange platform. The Objectif of this project is to centralize academic, professional and commercial exchanges.
